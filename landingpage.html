Get Started

How It Works

Step 1: Login & Redirect to Spotify

Click on the "Get Started" button to login and you will be redirected to Spotify to allow permissions.

Step 2: Allow Permission

Grant permission to access your Spotify data.

Step 3: Callback to Homepage

You will be redirected back to the homepage.

Step 4: Use Features

Explore features like viewing playlists, user profile, and getting personalized recommendations.

Key Features

Tailored to Your Taste

SongRecommender uses the Spotify API to analyze your listening habits and create personalized music recommendations.

Discover New Artists

Explore a diverse range of artists and genres that you may have never encountered before, expanding your musical horizons.

Evolving Recommendations

As you continue to listen and engage with the platform, the recommendations will adapt and improve, ensuring a constantly refreshed experience.

Quick Preview, Personalized Results

Listen to recommended music snippets for 30 seconds to quickly decide if it matches your taste, ensuring a personalized experience tailored to your preferences.

About SongRecommender

I got the idea for SongRecommender because I often listen to music on Spotify while coding. Finding new music that I like can be difficult sometimes, so I created this project to make it easier. SongRecommender uses the Spotify API to analyze your listening habits and provide personalized music recommendations. This project is also part of my portfolio for Holberton School.
